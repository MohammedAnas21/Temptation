Our Story

A regular's cafe,
built one order at a time.

Tucked opposite Bibi Raza Girls College in Khaja Colony, Temptations has become the kind of place people circle back to — not for one dish, but for the whole spread of burgers, momos, wraps and mojitos that keep the tables full from afternoon college breaks to late evening hangouts.

It's an easy, unfussy room: quick service on the busy days, a menu wide enough to please a table that can't agree on one thing, and a name that's earned a loyal, almost weekly, following across Kalaburagi.
